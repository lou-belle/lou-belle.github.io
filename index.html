Hi, welcome to my website. I'm Lou Belle, a high functioning South African lunatic, whom will unapologetically testify that I live in the most beautiful country in the world. Together with my husband - whom is annoyingly perfect - and our three feral farm kids, I live on a lovely farm that boasts some spectacular views. I'm not a farmer - life seems to take a sadistic type of pleasure in daily reminding me of this fact. Inspite of these challenges, I love it!

Heads up, I'm not a writer either, at least not technically. Infact I failed first grade, and was completely illiterate until my fifth grade remedial teacher completely infected me with a love of literature. By the end of that year I was reading my father's Wilber Smith novels and getting a whole different type of education. I will be eternally indebted to that teacher for all the worlds of wonder she opened the door upon. Today I am a qualified special needs educator, who for the moment has pressed pause on my career to educate my own children.

There are days, when home schooling and the isolation of the farm feel oppressively prohibative, and I desperately miss adult company and interaction - also having my own money. And so, dear reader, I made some up. Yes indeed, I have embarked upon the bold adventure of writing, I hope you'll join me.
